CSWDO Medical Assistance

Description:
A city government funded assistance program for clients needing assistance with medications.


Location:



Requirements:
1. Doctor’s Prescription with License number
2. Barangay clearance of the patient or parents if patient is minor
3. Voter’s ID or Botika sa Masa Card Fee Php 20.00 – Renewable once a year
4. Botika sa Masa Card
Note: If Botika sa Masa is available, no need to comply the first requirements


Steps:
1. Client applicant/family members proceed to City Social Welfare and Development Office to seek assistance
2. Interview clients/family member/Comply and submit requirements to the Social Worker assigned in the barangay/any Social Worker available
3. Issue Certificate of Eligibility and have it signed by City Social Welfare and Development Officer or representative
4. Record client and service provided in the log book
5. Record client and service provided in the log book
6. Proceed to City Health Office then to City Mayor’s Office for signature
7. Claim medicines at the City Health Pharmacy Cut off Time:
11:00 a.m.
04:00 p.m.
